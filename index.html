Thougth Bucket

Thought Bucket is a small social network I am building with PHP and MySQL. Users can think out loud by posting their most current thoughts. Then other users have the ability to upvote or downvote a thought depending on how they feel about it. Also, you can comment on thoughts that look intersting to you or even better that have crossed your mind as well!
